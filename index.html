Experience

Chelsea is a trainer with over 15 years of teaching experience specializing in Dressage and 3-Day Eventing. After many years spent competing as a young rider and then traveling the world to work and complete her education, she has made her home in the Pacific Northwest and trains part-time with a small group of wonderful clients. Her focus is on building a small client base of juniors and amateurs who want to grow their horse skills, focus on safety and mastery of concepts, and not lose sight of the fact that this horse thing is supposed to be fun! In addition to having significant show and competition experience, she is a trained educator with a background in teaching and learning.

  • Graduate Full B Pony Club Alumna
  • 4th Level Oregon State Jr/YR Dressage Champion
  • Competed through Preliminary level evening
  • Qualified for CCI*
  • Earned California Teaching Credential, Secondary Math
  • Managed teacher training programs for a range of organizations from non-profits to fast-growing education technology companies
